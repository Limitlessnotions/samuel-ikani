From Data to Decisions: Bridging the Policy Gap

Data is abundant. Decisions are not. The real challenge is translating data into action.

Across development programs, large volumes of data are collected but rarely used effectively. Reports are produced, dashboards are built, yet decision-making remains unchanged.

The Disconnect

Data systems are often disconnected from decision systems. This creates a situation where information exists, but it does not influence outcomes.

What Needs to Change

  • Align data collection with key decisions
  • Reduce unnecessary indicators
  • Prioritize clarity over complexity

Data becomes valuable only when it is embedded into decision workflows.
